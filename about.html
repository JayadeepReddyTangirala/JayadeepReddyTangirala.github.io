About Me

My Journey

I’ve never been able to explain the excitement I feel when faced with a challenge. From an early age, I instinctively chose the steepest climb instead of the easy trail—much to the bewilderment of those around me. I suppose it has always been in my nature to seek out the hardest path, determined to experience every twist and turn in my quest to truly understand the world.

Growing up, my curiosity took a very literal form. I couldn’t stop unboxing things—every toy, device, or gadget that found its way into my hands ended up in pieces. I was terrible at putting them back together, much to my sister’s amusement. She nicknamed me “Unboxing.” Little did she know this playful quip hinted at a deeper drive: the urge to uncover how everything fit and functioned beneath its surface. This small habit had far-reaching implications on my life, guiding me to ask questions and dig deeper, always driven by that “what if?” that echoes in the mind of a curious soul.

As I grew older, I realized that my desire to learn stretched beyond the confines of toys and household objects. I wanted to understand how even the most complex machines in the world worked—from intricate clocks and gears to the pinnacle of human engineering: rockets. My fascination with mechanisms and moving parts drew me to the broadest, oldest, and mightiest field: Mechanical Engineering. I was enthralled by every branch it offered. Thermodynamics sparked my imagination as it revealed the hidden dance of heat and energy transformation. Fluid Mechanics taught me the elegance of water and air, flowing so effortlessly yet governed by laws as unyielding as steel. And the wonder didn’t stop there—Design and Manufacturing processes showed me how raw materials morph into the tangible creations that shape our daily lives. Every discipline had its own story to tell, and I was determined to read them all.

With an unquenchable thirst for knowledge, I completed my Bachelor’s in Mechanical Engineering, proud yet eager to explore more. That eagerness led me to the United States, where I continued my studies and ventured deeper into my chosen field. I found myself specializing to a degree I never anticipated, working on projects so cutting-edge that my younger self—who once took apart clock radios—could never have imagined. The more I learned, the more I realized just how vast the world of engineering really is. There’s always a new theorem, a new discovery, or a new angle to explore.

That realization humbles me. No matter how many books I read or prototypes I design, there will always be something I don’t know—and that’s what keeps my inner “Unboxing” spirit alive. I’ve learned not to fear the unknown, but to embrace it, to open it up and see where it leads. This journey, from childhood tinkering to specialized engineering, has taught me that every question beckons with another door to open, another machine to examine, another story to unfold. And I, for one, can’t wait to see what I’ll discover next.

My Personality

People often ask me where my limitless curiosity comes from. I believe it started with that same childhood impulse to take everything apart—discovering how gears mesh or why certain parts fit together so perfectly. I carry that curiosity into my relationships and daily life, too. I’m always wondering what makes people tick, what their stories are, and how I can learn from them.

But it’s not just about gathering knowledge for me. Deep in my core, I hold a firm sense of justice and honesty. If I believe in something—truly believe it’s right—I stand by it without hesitation, even if there’s no one around to notice. I’ve learned that integrity is about doing the right thing in private, just as I would in public.

I’m also the kind of person who never meets a stranger. My friends say I could strike up a conversation with a statue, and it wouldn’t take long before we found common ground. There’s something magical about connecting with new people, forging friendships where none existed before. It’s taught me that everyone has a hidden chapter in their story, waiting to be discovered.

If you ever need help moving your furniture, deciphering a math problem, or just someone to talk to at three in the morning—I’m your person. Sometimes I do go a bit overboard in my efforts to help, but I’ve discovered that it’s worth it to see that spark of relief or gratitude in someone else’s eyes. I can’t help but be drawn to that feeling of shared humanity.

No matter how tough things get, I tend to see a silver lining. Optimism is woven into who I am. Whether it’s a new project or a crisis unfolding in real time, I believe every situation holds a lesson, an opportunity—even if it’s just a chance to stay calm amid chaos. During these high-pressure moments, I thrive. My mind clears, my thoughts sharpen, and I focus on solving the problem rather than panicking over it.

In the end, my personality circles back to that same spark of curiosity that defined me growing up: the drive to learn, to explore, to connect. People fascinate me. Challenges excite me. And hope guides me forward like a beacon on the horizon. That’s me in a nutshell—always eager, always ready for whatever lies around the next corner.

My Passion

The first thing that gets me out of bed every morning is the promise of discovery. I can’t help but feel a surge of excitement knowing that, by the day’s end, I’ll have learned something I didn’t know before. Whether it’s a hidden detail in a machine’s design or a fresh perspective gained from an unexpected conversation, the pursuit of knowledge drives me forward. Each morning, I tell myself: Be wiser today than you were yesterday. It’s a simple mantra, but it keeps me curious, energized, and ready to tackle whatever lies ahead.

Beyond mere curiosity, I’m deeply committed to leaving the world a little better than I found it. This may sound lofty, but I believe it doesn’t always take grand gestures—sometimes, it’s a matter of using the right words to lift someone’s spirits or an act of kindness that makes a moment easier for another person. I strive to weave this spirit into my daily life, not just my work. Being a better person at home and in my community is as important to me as any technological breakthrough I might contribute to as an engineer.

That said, mechanical engineering is the field that has captured my heart. Machines and processes aren’t just nuts and bolts; they’re puzzles waiting to be solved, systems that can be refined to improve lives on a global scale. When I design a new mechanism or optimize an existing process, I’m not just tinkering with metal and software—I’m pouring my passion into something tangible that might help someone travel safer, save energy, or bring clean water to remote places. Each new project becomes a bridge between my fascination with how things work and my desire to make the world a better place.

In the end, my passion for learning, helping, and engineering all converge into a single compass pointing me forward. Every piece of knowledge I gain fuels my ability to see the bigger picture and find new ways to contribute. Every step I take in my personal growth, every act of kindness I share, shapes who I am and underscores why I love what I do. My hope is that, bit by bit, the world becomes brighter not only through my professional achievements but also through the person I choose to be each day.

My Dream

My dream is to become the best version of myself, constantly expanding the boundaries of what I can achieve. To me, success isn’t just a destination—it’s a journey of steady improvement, fueled by curiosity and guided by a clear sense of purpose. Whether I’m devising a new engineering solution or simply learning a new skill, I measure my progress by how much I’ve grown compared to yesterday.

Mechanical engineering offers a world of possibilities for turning that dream into reality. Its breadth—from aerospace innovations to intricate manufacturing processes—means there’s always another challenge to conquer and another concept to master. I see each project as an opportunity to test my limits, refine my thinking, and raise the bar on what I’m capable of. In doing so, I believe I not only fulfill my own potential but also contribute something meaningful to the world at large.

Looking ahead, I envision a future where my work transcends boundaries and impacts people from all walks of life. I want to stand at the forefront of ingenuity, constantly seeking new ways to make technology safer, more efficient, and more accessible. But above all, I aim to do this with integrity, teamwork, and a commitment to never stop learning. By striving for my personal best, I hope to inspire those around me to do the same, creating an environment where everyone reaches heights they once only dreamed of.
